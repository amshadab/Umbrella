4.PARASOL UMBRELLA


A parasol umbrella is a type of umbrella that is specifically designed to provide shade from the sun. It usually features a large canopy made from lightweight and breathable materials such as cotton, silk, or nylon, and a long handle for easy carrying. Parasol umbrellas are often used for fashion purposes, such as completing an outfit or making a statement, rather than just for protection from the sun.
The term "parasol" comes from the Italian words "para" (to shield or protect) and "sol" (sun), which reflects the umbrella's primary purpose of protecting the user from the sun's harmful rays. Parasol umbrellas are available in a variety of styles, designs, and colors, from classic vintage styles to more modern and trendy options.
In addition to their practical use, parasol umbrellas can also serve as a decorative accessory for weddings, photo shoots, and other outdoor events. They are typically more delicate and lightweight than traditional umbrellas, and are often used in conjunction with other outdoor accessories such as sun hats, sunglasses, and beach towels.
Some popular brands that offer parasol umbrellas include B&Y, Kate Spade, and Totes. Prices can vary depending on the brand, size, and design, but generally range from $20 to $100 or more.
