1.STRAIGHT UMBRELLA


A straight umbrella is a traditional type of umbrella that features a straight, long handle and a canopy that is supported by metal ribs. It is the most common type of umbrella and is designed to be easy to use and provide basic protection against rain and sunlight.
Straight umbrellas come in a variety of sizes, ranging from compact versions that are easy to carry in a purse or backpack, to larger versions that can provide coverage for multiple people. They are also available in a wide range of colors and designs, from classic solid colors to fun patterns and prints.
One of the main advantages of a straight umbrella is its simplicity and durability. They are relatively inexpensive, easy to operate, and can withstand moderate wind and rain. However, they may not be the best option for extreme weather conditions, as their design may make them more vulnerable to gusts of wind.
In summary, a straight umbrella is a classic and reliable option for basic rain and sun protection. Its versatility and affordability make it a popular choice for everyday use.
