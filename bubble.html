7.BUBBLE UMBRELLA


A bubble umbrella is a unique type of rain umbrella that features a clear, dome-shaped canopy that creates a bubble-like effect around the user's head and upper body. The canopy is typically made of plastic or PVC, and is designed to provide a wide range of visibility and protection from the rain.
One of the main benefits of a bubble umbrella is that it provides more coverage than a traditional umbrella. Because the canopy is domed and wider than a regular umbrella, it can protect the user from rain that may be coming in from different angles. Additionally, the clear canopy allows the user to see through it, which can be especially helpful when walking in crowded areas or crossing busy streets.
Bubble umbrellas are also known for their unique and stylish design. They come in a variety of colors and patterns, and can be a fun way to add a pop of personality to your rainy day wardrobe.
While bubble umbrellas are primarily used as rain umbrellas, they can also be used to provide shade on sunny days. The clear canopy allows light to come through while still providing some protection from the sun's harmful rays.
Overall, a bubble umbrella can be a stylish and practical accessory to add to your rainy day arsenal.
