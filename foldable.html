2.FOLDABLE UMBRELLA


Foldable umbrellas are compact and convenient, making them a popular choice for people who want to carry an umbrella with them on-the-go. They usually come with a small carrying case or sleeve that can easily fit into a purse, backpack, or briefcase. Foldable umbrellas can be further classified into two types: manual and automatic.
Manual foldable umbrellas require manual effort to open and close the umbrella. They usually have a simple button or latch mechanism that needs to be operated manually to extend or collapse the canopy.
On the other hand, automatic foldable umbrellas use a motorized mechanism to open and close the umbrella. They have a button or switch that, when pressed, triggers the mechanism to open the canopy automatically.
The price of foldable umbrellas can vary depending on their quality, features, and brand. Generally, manual foldable umbrellas can range from around $10 to $50, while automatic foldable umbrellas can range from around $20 to $100 or more. Designer brands or specialized features such as UV protection or wind resistance can also significantly affect the price.
